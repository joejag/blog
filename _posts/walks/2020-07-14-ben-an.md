- Where: [Ben A'an](https://www.walkhighlands.co.uk/lochlomond/ben-aan.shtml)
- Region: Trossacks
- With: Adrian & Ania
- Ascent: 340m
- Distance: 3.7km
- Time: 90 minutes

### Climbed

- Ben A'an - 454m

### Notes

- I can climb a hill!
- I don't have very good balance, going downhill was hard mentally
- Arrive before 8:30 if you want a car parking space

### To improve

- Never over extend your knee if you can help it
- Remember to stretch at the start and end
